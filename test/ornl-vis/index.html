ORNL Data Visualization
Visual Analytics + SciVis + InfoVis
ORNL researchers design, implement, and practically evaluate data visualization techinques to enable knowledge discovery in large and complex data. These techniques are integrated into innovative systems that span the full data science life cycle and are applied to a wide range of domains of national significance, such as materials science, cyber security, urban dynamics, climate science, and advanced manufacturing.
