ORNL Data Visualization
Visual Analytics + SciVis + InfoVis
ORNL visualization researchers focus on designing, implementing, and evaluating new data visualization techniques that enable knowledge discovery from large and complex data. The visual representations that we create take advantage of the unmatched pattern matching capabilities of the human visual system. Partnering with leading experts, we apply these techniques to a wide range of domains of national significance, such as advanced manufacturing, cyber security, physics, urban dynamics, intelligence, chemistry, health care, climate science, and materials science.
